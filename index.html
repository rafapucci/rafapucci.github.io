PogTeam - CTF Team

WTF is CTF?
Capture the Flag (CTF) is a special kind of information security competitions. There are three common types of CTFs: Jeopardy, Attack-Defence and mixed.

PogTeam
A Brazilian CTF team. Created by a group of information security enthusiasts, the team aims to share experiences among the members and, of course, participate in CTF events for fun!
